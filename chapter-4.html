第四章 ⚠️ 含不可错过成就

烈火

大规模战斗 · 火焰BT · 限时救援成就

📋 详细流程
1. 护送车队穿越燃烧的油田，注意躲避坠落物
2. 使用消防背包扑灭路障火焰（按住R1/RB喷射灭火剂）
3. BOSS战：火焰BT，弱点在其背后的燃料罐
4. 【限时成就】在10分钟内救出3名被困NPC
5. 击败火焰BT后，穿越废墟抵达新区域
6. 剧情动画揭示幕后黑手，解锁新装备「热成像护目镜」
7. 完成本章后获得消防背包升级版
1. Escort convoy through burning oil field, avoid falling debris
2. Use Firefighter Pack to extinguish roadblock flames (hold R1/RB to spray)
3. Boss fight: Flame BT, weak point is fuel tank on back
4. [Timed Trophy] Rescue 3 trapped NPCs within 10 minutes
5. After defeating Flame BT, cross ruins to reach new area
6. Cutscene reveals mastermind, unlock 'Thermal Goggles'
7. Obtain upgraded Firefighter Pack after chapter
可获得物品：
  • 消防背包（Lv.1）
  • 热成像护目镜
  • 火焰BT残骸x1
  • 被困NPC奖励包x3
  • 开罗尔晶体x15
Items:
  • Firefighter Pack (Lv.1)
  • Thermal Goggles
  • Flame BT Remnant x1
  • Trapped NPC Reward Pack x3
  • Chiral Crystal x15
⚔️ BOSS战攻略 · 火焰BT
📊 阶段分解：
  • 阶段1 - 地面战：躲避火焰喷射，绕到背后攻击燃料罐
  • 阶段2 - 狂暴模式：全身着火，需要先用消防背包灭火才能造成伤害
🎒 推荐装备： 消防背包、血液手雷x10、远程武器
💡 特殊机制： BOSS每次喷火后会短暂停顿，此时是最佳攻击时机。场景中有多个水桶可以熄灭身上的火。
⚔️ Boss Guide · Flame BT
📊 Phases:
  • Phase 1 - Ground battle: Dodge flame spray, go behind to attack fuel tank
  • Phase 2 - Enraged mode: Body on fire, must extinguish with Firefighter Pack first to deal damage
🎒 Recommended Gear: Firefighter Pack, Hemogrenade x10, Ranged Weapon
💡 Special Mechanics: BOSS pauses briefly after each flame spray, best attack window. Multiple water buckets in arena to extinguish fire on self.
🔍 隐藏物品/彩蛋：
  • 隐藏道具「油田工程师的日志」：油田东侧废弃办公室内，可解锁消防背包强化蓝图
🔍 Hidden Items/Easter Eggs:
  • Hidden item 'Oil Field Engineer's Log': In abandoned office east of oil field, unlocks Firefighter Pack upgrade BP
🏆 本章成就/奖杯：
  • 🏆 烈火英雄（银奖） - 在10分钟内救出3名NPC（不可错过）
  • 🏆 灭火专家（铜奖） - 不使用消防背包击败火焰BT
  • 🏆 完美护送（铜奖） - 护送车队零伤亡
🏆 Trophies/Achievements:
  • 🏆 Fire Hero (Silver) - Rescue 3 NPCs within 10 minutes (Missable)
  • 🏆 Firefighting Expert (Bronze) - Defeat Flame BT without Firefighter Pack
  • 🏆 Perfect Escort (Bronze) - Complete convoy with zero casualties
🎮 平台差异提示：
PS5: 消防背包喷射时有自适应扳机阻力反馈；PC: 鼠标瞄准更精准，适合挑战「灭火专家」成就。
🎮 Platform Differences:
PS5: Adaptive trigger resistance feedback when using Firefighter Pack; PC: Mouse aiming more precise for 'Firefighting Expert' trophy.
⚠️ 警告
⚠️ 第四章救援任务是限时成就，推进前务必存档！从触发救援任务开始计时10分钟，错过需二周目。建议提前熟悉NPC位置。
⚠️ Warning
⚠️ Chapter 4 rescue mission is timed trophy! Save before progressing. 10-minute timer starts when rescue triggers, miss requires NG+. Familiarize NPC locations beforehand.
